Reverse Seared Steak

Ingredients

  • Big Ol' Steaks
  • Steak Seasoning
  • Butta

Directions

  1. Season both side of steak & set in fridge for a coupla hours
  2. Cook in oven for 1 hour at two hunned degrees
  3. Heat up some butta in a thickbottom pan
  4. Sear steak in pan, 1 minute for each side
  5. Slice and gorge yourself
